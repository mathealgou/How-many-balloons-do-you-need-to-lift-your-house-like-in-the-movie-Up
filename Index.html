How Many Balloons do you need to lift your house like in the movie Up?

The lift of a single balloon
Although helium weighs twice as much hydrogen, because each gas is so much lighter than air helium provides about 93% of hydrogen's lift at full purity. In practical operation it is impossible to achieve or maintain 100% purity of either gas, reducing helium's lifting ability to about 88% of the lift of hydrogen.
According to airships.net.

The weight of a house
The general rule of thumb surrounding the construction industry is that a single-level home weighs roughly 200 pounds per square foot (976 kilos per square meter).
According to upgradedhome.com.
